Return / Refund / Cancellation Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase from familybookstore Books's. If you have any questions about our refund, return, and cancellation policy, please don’t hesitate to reach out to us.

  • You have 3 days to return / replace / request a refund from the date you received the item.
  • The item must be unused and in the same condition as received.
  • The item must be in its original packaging.

Customers must record an unpacking video clearly showing any defect or damage. This video is mandatory for raising any return, replacement, or refund request. Requests without an unpacking video will not be accepted.

Damages Covered Under Policy

  1. Severe spine damage causing pages to come apart
  2. In-transit damage (minor corner bends, dents, or cover scratches are not covered)
  3. More than 2 misprinted pages
  4. More than 2 torn pages
  5. More than 2 missing pages
  6. Smudged pages making content unreadable (more than 2 pages)

Note: Box sets like Harry Potter or Lord of the Rings include delicate outer boxes. Damage to the outer box is not covered. Partial refunds may be provided if 1–2 books have printing errors. Full set replacement is only possible if all books and the box are damaged during transit. Pre-loved books are not eligible for returns or replacements.

Refund Process

  • No automatic refunds are available.
  • Customers must contact us via WhatsApp for refunds on prepaid returned orders.
  • Returned items will be inspected before approval.
  • Refunds are initiated within 1–2 days after receiving the return.
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method.

Exchanges

Please contact us if you wish to exchange an item.

Cancellation Policy

  • Orders can be cancelled within 5 hours of placing the order.
  • Orders already shipped or in transit cannot be cancelled.
  • Full refund is provided for eligible cancellations.
  • Orders cannot be modified after placing.
  • Address and contact details cannot be changed after shipment.

Shipping & Returns

Our courier partner will pick up the return from your doorstep. We bear the shipping cost for returns and replacements.
